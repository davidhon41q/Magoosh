## What is Magoosh? Is It Worth It?

Magoosh is an online test prep platform founded in 2009 by Hansoo Lee, Bhavin Parikh, and Pejman Negin, based in Berkeley, California. The company was built on a single founding principle: that quality test preparation should be affordable and accessible to every student, not just those who can afford expensive classroom courses or private tutors. Over fifteen years, Magoosh has grown to serve over 10 million students across the globe and is consistently rated among the top online test prep platforms on Trustpilot (4.6/5) and across major review forums.

What Makes Magoosh Different

Magoosh's defining competitive advantage is price-to-quality ratio. While Kaplan and Princeton Review classroom courses for the GMAT or LSAT commonly cost $1,000–$2,000, Magoosh's equivalent plans range from $129 to $600 at full price — and are frequently available at 20–30% off with active promo codes. Despite the lower price, Magoosh's LSAT courses, for example, are taught entirely by 99th percentile scorers, and the GMAT course has been independently benchmarked as delivering comparable score improvements to far more expensive alternatives.

The platform's most distinctive content feature is video explanations for every single practice question — not just the ones you answer incorrectly. This means every practice session doubles as a learning session, regardless of whether you get questions right or wrong. Most competing platforms only provide explanations for incorrect answers. Magoosh's approach produces a more thorough understanding of each question type and the reasoning required to answer it under time pressure.

Standout Features of Magoosh

Magoosh's score predictor tool (available on GRE and select other courses) is one of the most valued features among students. It analyses your practice performance across question type, difficulty, and timing to estimate your likely score range on the official exam — giving you a data-driven sense of where you stand and how much more preparation you need before sitting.

The personalised study schedule generator is another standout. You enter your exam date and available daily study hours, and Magoosh builds a day-by-day study plan that sequences lessons and practice sets to maximise score improvement within your available time. The plan adjusts dynamically as you progress.

Magoosh's mobile apps (iOS and Android) are highly rated and support full offline access — lessons can be downloaded and practised without an internet connection, making Magoosh well-suited to commuters, travellers, and students in regions with inconsistent connectivity.

Is Magoosh Worth It?

For the vast majority of test prep needs — GRE, GMAT, SAT, ACT, TOEFL, LSAT, or MCAT — Magoosh represents outstanding value, particularly when promo codes reduce the already competitive base price by 20–30%. The combination of affordable pricing, the 7-day free trial, and the score improvement guarantee on most plans means there is minimal financial risk in trying the platform.

The primary limitation to be honest about: Magoosh does not offer live tutoring outside its GMAT Live and LSAT Live plans. For students who need real-time, personalised instruction or have unconventional preparation needs, Magoosh's self-paced model may require supplementing with additional resources. For the majority of motivated, self-directed students, however, Magoosh's curriculum, question bank, and adaptive tools are more than sufficient to achieve meaningful score improvements.
